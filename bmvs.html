Welcome to Burnpur Mahila Voluntary Samity

Burnpur Mahila Voluntary Samity is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering women and promoting sustainable livelihoods. Our mission is to provide a platform for women to develop their skills, gain economic independence, and improve their overall well-being.

Our Products

Handmade Crafts

Our handmade crafts are made with love and care by our skilled artisans. Each piece is unique and reflects the beauty of traditional craftsmanship.

Organic Food Products

Our organic food products are made from the finest ingredients, sourced from local farmers. Our products are healthy, delicious, and sustainable.
